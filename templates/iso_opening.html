ISO

ISO is a number that represents how sensitive your camera sensor is to light. The higher the ISO number, the more sensitive your camera is, meaning you need less light to take a picture. ISO performance also depends on the size of your camera sensor — cameras with larger sensors (like full-frame sensors) generally perform better, maintaining image quality even at higher ISO levels.

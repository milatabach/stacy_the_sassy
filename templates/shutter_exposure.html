Shutter Speed and Exposure

Shutter speed directly affects the exposure of your photograph. A slower shutter speed lets in more light, making the image brighter, while a faster shutter speed lets in less light, making the image darker.

Choosing the right shutter speed helps balance the exposure depending on the lighting conditions and the effect you want to achieve.
